## Lagrange's Theorem
Let $G$ be a finite group and let $H$ be a subgroup of G. Then $$|G|/|H| = [G:H]$$ si the number of distinct left cosets of $H$ in $G$. In particular, the number of elements in $H$ must divide the number of elements in $G$.
